We are thrilled to be participating in the @groovyweb AI Agent Hackathon! 🚀 Over the last two days, we’ve been building something to fix a major pain point developers face every day. #AIAgentHackathon

🔴 **The Problem:** 
On large client projects, developers spend hours context-switching just to fix minor bugs. Fetching the ticket, hunting down files, writing the fix, and creating a PR easily eats up 2-3 hours per issue.

💡 **The Solution:**
Meet our **Autonomous Bug Solver**. 

🤖 **The Agent:**
Instead of a human doing the grunt work, our multi-agent orchestration system takes over. You feed it a ticket ID, and it autonomously reads the issue, locates the broken files using a Context Agent, writes the fix, and automatically opens a Pull Request! 

📈 **The Impact:**
By completely automating the context gathering and PR creation pipeline, we are estimating this agent will save developers over 15 hours a week. Less time hunting bugs, more time building features.
